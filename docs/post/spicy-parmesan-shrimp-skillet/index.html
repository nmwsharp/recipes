Spicy Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

Shrimp and pasta

  • 1 pound raw peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 1⁄4 cup olive oil
  • 1⁄8 cup parmesan cheese
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 package pasta (linguine are my favorite)
  • sliced green onion (garnish)

Cream sauce:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp parmesan cheese
  • red pepper flakes
  • cayenne pepper
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

In a bowl, whisk olive oil, parmesan, brown sugar, soy sauce, garlic and red pepper flakes together. Add shrimp to a large ziplock bag, pour marinade over top, then seal bag and shake to coat. Place in the fridge to marinate for at least 30 minutes, up to overnight.

When ready to eat, bring water to boil in a large pot and cook pasta according to directions. While pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp and marinade (just dump the entire contents of the bag into the skillet), then cook shrimp until pink â€“ about 3-4 minutes per side. If you cook for too long, the brown sugar from the marinade will start to blacken and smoke, this is bad! Once the shrimp are done, remove them and make the cream sauce in the pot, combining all ingredients and cooking over low heat until the sauce has reduced by a third. Serve shrimp over pasta and top with cream sauce.

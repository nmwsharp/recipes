Rustic Tomato Pasta

Started from: http://imgur.com/a/wB267

3x15 oz can whole, peeled tomatoes
1 med/large onion, finely chopped
5 cloves garlic
~1/3 oz each fresh oregano/parsley/basil
2 bay leaves
red pepper flakes
cayenne pepper
olive oil

Chop onion and garlic. Cook in pot with some oil until clear (don't brown).

Add in other ingredients, including juice from tomato cans. Once it's cook a bit, use a wooden spoon to break up the tomatoes. Cook for ~30 min or until it looks yummy.

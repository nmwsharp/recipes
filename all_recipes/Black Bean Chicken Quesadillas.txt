Black Bean Chicken Quesadillas

Adapted from http://www.budgetbytes.com/2012/02/hearty-black-bean-quesadillas/

Ingredients:

1/2 lb, chicken tenderloin (+oil to cook, other cuts are fine)
1 (15 oz.) can black beans
1 cup frozen corn kernels
1/2 small red onion (diced)
1 clove garlic (minced)
1/2 bunch fresh cilantro (chopped)
2 cups shredded cheese
1 batch taco seasoning (try chili powder and cayenne)
fresh squeezed lime (to taste)

10 taco sized tortillas

Feeds ~3 hungry college students.

Instructions:

Cook chicken with some garlic and oil. Chop up everything else.
Mix everything together in a giant bowl. Make quesidillas in a skillet. Wasn't that easy?

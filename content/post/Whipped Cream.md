## Ingredients

Bread:

- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 2 Tbsp powdered sugar (or normal white sugar)
- 1/2 tsp vanilla

## Directions

Combine all ingredients Whip at high speed until peaks form, about 1-3 min.

Keeps for ~3 days in fridge.
